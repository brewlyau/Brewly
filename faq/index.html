FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Does Brewly cost anything?

The Brewly app is 100% free to download and use. You only pay for your coffee subscriptions - there are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no charges just to use the app.

How much can I actually save?

Depending on the cafe, you can save anywhere up to 50% on your regular coffee order. The more you subscribe, the more you save.

How do I redeem my coffee?

Simply open the app, swipe to redeem, and show the screen to your barista. They'll confirm it visually and make your drink.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. Pause or cancel your subscription whenever you like, no questions asked.

Can I use my coffees at any cafe?

Your subscription is tied to the cafe you choose. You can have multiple subscriptions at different cafes if you like.

What coffees can I get?

Each cafe sets their own menu for Brewly subscribers. Most offer their standard coffee menu at the discounted rate.

How do I find cafes near me?

Download the app and browse the map to see all participating cafes in your area.

When am I charged?

You're charged upfront when you subscribe, then at the start of each new billing cycle (weekly, fortnightly, or monthly depending on your plan).

What if a cafe closes or leaves Brewly?

If your cafe leaves the platform, we'll notify you immediately and refund any unused coffees from your current subscription.
